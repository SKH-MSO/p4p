# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic principles and tokenized values of our design system.

## Color Palette

Our palette is inspired by earthy tones and sophisticated neutrals, aiming for a classic and understated elegance.

- **Primary Color:** `#A68966` - A warm, inviting tan, reminiscent of aged leather or natural wood.
- **Secondary Color:** `#4B3D33` - A deep, rich brown, providing strong contrast and grounding for the palette.
- **Tertiary Color:** `#F5F5F0` - A soft, creamy off-white, offering a gentle background and highlight.
- **Neutral Color:** `#FAF9F6` - A crisp, clean white, serving as the primary background for most surfaces.
- **Color Mode:** `light` - The system is optimized for bright interfaces.

## Typography

Our typography balances readability with a touch of modern sophistication.

- **Headlines:** `manrope` - Clean, modern, and highly legible, perfect for impactful headings.
- **Body Text:** `workSans` - A versatile and friendly sans-serif, ensuring comfortable reading for longer passages.
- **Labels:** `workSans` - Consistent with body text for clear and concise labeling across the UI.

## Shape & Form

The physical appearance of our UI elements.

- **Roundedness:** `1` - Subtle rounded corners provide a touch of softness without compromising structure, creating a modern yet approachable feel.

## Spacing

The arrangement and density of our UI elements.

- **Spacing:** `2` - Normal spacing ensures a balanced layout, providing sufficient breathing room between elements for clarity and ease of use, without feeling overly sparse or cramped.
